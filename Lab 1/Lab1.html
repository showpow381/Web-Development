10/4/22: Best Waifu

Ok lets address the facts here....every anime has one girl that the creaters were like "yep everyone will love her" And yep I'm guilty of it too. Either that or they were just going crazy with creating the girls in the anime. Regardless, here is the one anime girl or "waifu" that stuck out the most to me. In an Isekai show called Re;Zero there is a twin girl that is a maid for the main girls house. The maids name is Rem and she is a blue hair Demon that has the power to destory anything in her path when she is enraged. Also her twin sister is named Ram and she is a Demon as well, she just lost her powers. The short story behind that is the village that they lived in was attacked and her sister as a baby was ripped of her horn, thus she lost her powers.
